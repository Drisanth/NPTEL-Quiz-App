# NPTEL HCI Practice Quiz App

Welcome to the NPTEL Human Computer Interaction Practice Quiz!

## Practice Options
- **Practice Mode:** Provides immediate feedback on whether your answer is correct.
- **Test Mode:** Simulates a real test where feedback is only shown at the end.
- **Question Selection:** Choose to practice by specific weeks (1-12) or attempt all 120 questions.
- **Customizable Options:** Toggle question shuffling, show/hide score, and dark mode.

All the best for your preparations! Hope this helps you ace your NPTEL exams.
